What is MetaCloset

MetaCloset contains a wide array of NFT versions of fashion items, starting off with sneakers and potentially expanding to designer clothes, sports team gear, jewelry, and more.

Our NFT includes the base photos of popular sneakers at different angles, and then transformed into art through different filters at every angle. An NFT is a combination of all frames of a sneaker to create an animated spin, with each angle of the sneaker being unique.

Incentives of buying from MetaCloset versus other NFTs:

  • An exclusive community
  • Eco-friendly blockchain and extremely cheap gas fees
  • High chances at winning free rewards and exclusive events for MetaCloset members

Three high school students from the Peddie school were extremely intrigued by the cross-application of computer science, business & entrepreneurship, and more. Outside of the classroom, they are intrigued by fashion, and have all dreamt of launching their own NFT. Given the chance now, they hope to build a large community with MetaCloset, and are heavily invested in making the art and the community special.
